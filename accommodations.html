Accommodations

We have requested a group discount rate with Rocky Mountain Resorts for our wedding guests. If at least seven units are booked with our group, those guests will receive a 5% discount off each night and apply to all nights of everyone’s reservations. The discount is applied about a week prior to your reservation.

Our group name is “Tietze-Iza wedding”. If you book online, mention in the “Special Request” section that you are with the Tietze-Iza wedding. If you reserve over the phone, mention this to the reservation desk.

Rocky Mountain Resorts has six locations in Estes Park, as well as vacation homes throughout Estes Park. Each has its own unique features. Most feature kitchens, and fireplaces. Some have personal hot tubs or in-room Jacuzzi tubs. You can book any available unit at any time and they are reserved on a first come first serve availability.

To view their units, visit: http://www.rockymtnresorts.com/
